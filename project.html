NICE AND COMFORTABLE PLACE TO STAY

Our Leisure Inn. provide comfortable air-cooled hostel accommodation separately for boys and girls in the campus. The hostels are well equipped with all the modern amenities and are under the charge of caring and experienced Wardens. Special features like ultramodern gymnasium and sports facilities, internet center, 24 hrs internet connectivity in the rooms through WI-FI, solar water heating system, recreation room, drinking water treatment plant, generators for power backup and tight security arrangements ensure a pleasant stay allowing students to focus on academics. Special efforts are made to provide homely, hygienic and nourishing food to the students and a separate library is attached to the hostel.
